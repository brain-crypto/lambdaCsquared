This tool is designed to help you circumvent WeChat's content filter by adding random characters. It is optimised for Chinese characters.

Instruction:
1. Enter your text in the area below
2. Click "Convert"
3. Select your preferences (optional)
4. copy paste the text, or send a screenshot of the text and background (safer)

Brief explanation:
As of 2022, WeChat's filter simply scans messages for censored words. It can be easily countered by spreading the characters apart.
Meanwhile, adding a generic, artistic background helps against WeChat's similar image filter

For more information or other methods of circumventing WeChat filter, see the linked github repo.
